About Our Kgotla

Tsitsing Kgotla ya Rakhudu stands as a beacon of traditional governance and cultural preservation in our community. For generations, we have served as the meeting place where wisdom is shared, decisions are made, and our rich heritage is celebrated.

Our Kgotla operates under the guidance of traditional leaders who carry forward the customs and values passed down through centuries. We welcome all community members - elders who hold our stories, youth seeking to understand their roots, and cultural groups dedicated to preserving our way of life.

Here, every voice matters, every tradition is honored, and every gathering strengthens the bonds that unite us as one people.
